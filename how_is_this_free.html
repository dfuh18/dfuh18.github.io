Zero costs

Thank you for your interest in our app.
To use our text generation service, we partner with OpenAI that provides the underlying technology. As such, we require all users to create an account with OpenAI in order to obtain their own API key, which is necessary for using our service.
This allows us to ensure that our users have access to the latest and most reliable technology, while also giving us the ability to provide our services for free and improve our service over time. Creating an account with OpenAI is quick, easy and most of all 100% free, and allows us to provide you with a high-quality and reliable service.
We appreciate your understanding and look forward to helping you study.
