Hey there!
Thank you so much for checking out StudyMateAI.

If you are not sure on how StudyMateAI works or need a showcase of the features, feel free to explore on this page. :)

Installed but nothing happens?
If you installed our extension in your browser of choice and wonder where to find it, fear not! It can be accessed through three different ways:

 1. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar (see the icon in the bottom left/right? Try to hover over it with your mouse). Tipp: The position can be changed in the settings.

 2. Open the StudyMateAI popup (default shortcut: ctrl + shift + x). Tipp: Try the shortcut when its minimized.

 3. Access StudyMateAI via your browsers extensions menu at the top right. Maybe you already spottet our icon? In Chrome, click the small puzzle piece and then StudyMateAI.
  Tipp: Use the pin icon next to our extension to create a shortcut in the top right.

How to use StudyMateAI to get fast and simple explanations.
It is fairly easy:

 1. Select the word you want further explanation on. This could be a single word, a phrase or the name of a concept.
 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAI' and choose 'Definition'
 3. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar or your popup (default shortcut: ctrl + shift + x)
 4. Click 'Send request'

How to use StudyMateAI to make complex text comprehensive.
It is pretty straight forward:

 1. Select the text you want to (short) summarize. Please note that we can currently handle requests with a maximum of 1000 words
 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAI' and choose 'Summarize Selection' or 'Short Summary'
 3. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar or your popup (default shortcut: ctrl + shift + x)
 4. Click 'Send request'
