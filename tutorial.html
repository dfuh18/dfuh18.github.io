How to:

How to use StudyMateAi to make complex text comprehensive.
It is fairly easy:

 1. Select the word you want further explanation on. This could be a single word, a phrase or the name of a concept.
 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAi' and choose 'Definition'
 3. Open StudyMateAi via the magic sidebar or your popup (default shortcut: ctrl + shift + x)
 4. Click 'Send request'
