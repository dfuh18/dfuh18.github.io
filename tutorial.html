How to use StudyMateAI's Features

Definition

Fast and Simple explanations

This feature explains the meaning of a word, phrase, or concept. It provides a clear and concise explanation of what something is or means, and helps to establish a common understanding of the term being defined. To get the definition of the selected text follow these simple steps:

 1. Select the word you want further explanation on.
  This could be a single word, a phrase or the name of a concept.

 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAI' and choose 'Definition'.

 3. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar or your popup.

 4. Click 'Send request'.

Summarize

Make complex text comprehensive

This feature provides you with a concise and clear understanding of the key points or main ideas of the original text. You can choose between a longer version, which will tend to paraphrase shorter texts or reduce the input to its fundamentals.

 1. Select the text you want to (short) summarize.
  Please note that we can currently handle requests with a maximum of 1000 words.

 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAI' and choose 'Summarize Selection' or
  'Short Summary'.

 3. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar or your popup.

 4. Click 'Send request'


Bulletpoints

Ready to use in presentations and summaries

Bullet points are a type of formatting used to present information in a concise and organized way. They are typically used to highlight key points or important information in a list format.

 1. Select the text you want to summarize in bulletpoints.
  Please note that we can currently handle requests with a maximum of 1000 words.

 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAI' and choose 'Create Bulletpoints'.

 3. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar or your popup.

 4. Click 'Send request'

Answer Question

Answer practise questions or enter your own prompts
This feature can be used in different ways, try to enter your own questions into the intput field. The 'Answer Question' option is sort of a wildcard, but to use its main function follow these steps:

 1. Select the question you want the answer to.

 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAI' and choose 'Answer Question'.

 3. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar or your popup.

 4. Click 'Send request'


Create Flashcards

Prepare your next study session

Flashcards can be a simple but effective way to improve memory and retention of information. They typically consist of a set of cards, with a question or prompt on one side and an answer on the other side. To extract the essential features from any text and prepare some flashcards, follow these steps:

 1. Select the text you want flashcards of.

 2. Right click and navigate to 'StudyMateAI' and choose 'Create Flashcards'.

 3. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar or your popup.

 4. Click 'Send request'.

Installed but nothing happens?
If you installed our extension in your browser of choice and wonder where to find it, fear not! It can be accessed through three different ways:

 1. Open StudyMateAI via the magic sidebar (see the icon in the bottom left/right? Try to hover over it with your mouse). Tipp: The position can be changed in the settings.

 2. Open the StudyMateAI popup (default shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+8). Tipp: Try the shortcut when its minimized.

 3. Access StudyMateAI via your browsers extensions menu at the top right. Maybe you already spottet our icon? In Chrome, click the small puzzle piece and then StudyMateAI.
  Tipp: Use the pin icon next to our extension to create a shortcut in the top right.
